How to make a peanut butter and jelly sandwich

  1. Collect the ingredients/tools!
    • 2 pieces of bread
    • Peanut butter
    • Jelly or jam
    • Butter knife (not for consumption)
  2. Spread the peanut butter evenly onto one slice of bread using the butter knife.
  3. Spread jelly or jam evenly onto the other slice of bread using the butter knife.
  4. Press the two slices of bread together.
  5. Enjoy!
